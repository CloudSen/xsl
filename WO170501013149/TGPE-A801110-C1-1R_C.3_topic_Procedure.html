  1. Removal of Starter Magnetic Plug for Inspection.
    拆下起动机磁堵做检查
    1. Remove the knurled magnetic plug (90) from oil drain plug (110):
      从滑油放油塞(110)拆下带凸边的磁堵（90）
      1. Push and turn counter-clockwise to release the plug (90).
        按下并逆时针旋转以取出磁堵（90）

        NOTE:The oil drain plug (110) has a built-in check valve to permit removal of magnetic plug (90) without draining oil from the starter.

        注意:滑油放油塞（110）有一个内置的单向阀可以允许拆下磁堵（90）时起动机的滑油不会排出
