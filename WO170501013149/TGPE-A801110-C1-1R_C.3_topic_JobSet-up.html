  1. Safety Precautions
    安全措施
    1. On the center pedestal, on the ENG panel 115VU:
      在中央操纵台发动机面板115VU:
      1. Put a WARNING NOTICE(S) to tell persons not to start the engine.
        放置一个警告标牌以告戒他人不要启动发动机
    2. Make sure that the engine 1(2) shutdown occurred not less than 5 minutes before you do this procedure.
      执行程序前,确认发动机1(2)号已关车至少5分钟
    3. On the overhead maintenance panel 50VU:
      在头顶维护面板50VU
      1. Make sure that the ON legend of the ENG/FADEC GND PWR/1(2) pushbutton switch is off.
        确认ENG/FADEC GND PWR1/(2)按扭的ON灯灭
      2. Put a WARNING NOTICE(S) to tell persons not to energize the FADEC 1(2).
        放置警告牌告诫其他人员不要接通FADEC1(2)
