SUBTASK 52-10-00-010-079-A

授权：A320-214机型航线维护（限制项目）
工作：工作者：工作时间：
  1. Get Access
    接近

    1. WARNING:DO NOT OPEN THE PASSENGER/CREW DOOR IF THE WIND SPEED IS MORE THAN 65 KNOTS.

      警告:风速在65节以上时，不要打开旅客/机组门。

      WARNING:BEFORE YOU START WORK ON THE DOOR, MAKE SURE THAT:
      -  THE EMERGENCY CONTROL HANDLE IS IN THE DISARMED POSITION WITH THE SAFETY PIN INSTALLED.
      -  THE PERCUSSION LEVER OF THE DOOR-DAMPER AND EMERGENCY-OPERATION CYLINDER IS IN THE DISARMED POSITION WITH THE SAFETY PIN INSTALLED.

      警告:在对舱门工作前，应确认：
      -  应急操纵手柄在DISARMED位，并插有安全销。
      -  门阻尼器和应急作动筒的作动杆位于DISARMED位，并插有安全销。

      WARNING:STOP THE OPENING PROCEDURE IF THE RED WARNING LIGHT FLASHES. RESIDUAL PRESSURE COULD CAUSE THE DOOR TO OPEN WITH A SUDDEN FORCE AND INJURE PERSONS AND/OR DAMAGE THE AIRCRAFT.

      警告:如果红色警告灯闪亮，应停止开门程序。余压可能会使舱门突然受力而打开，这会造成人员受伤和/或飞机受损。

      Put the ACCESS PLATFORM 4M (13 FT)-ADJUSTABLE in position in front of the passenger/crew door 831 or 841 or 832 or 842.
      放置 4米（13英寸）可调接近平台到旅客/机组门 831或 841或 832或 842的前面。
    2. Open the passenger/crew door . (ALLRef.TASK. 52-10-00-010-001-A ).
      打开旅客/机组门 。 (ALL参考.TASK. 52-10-00-010-001-A )。
    3. Obey the special precautions before you start work on the door . (ALLRef.TASK. 52-10-00-860-001-A ).
      对舱门工作前，遵循特定的安全规范 。 (ALL参考.TASK. 52-10-00-860-001-A )。
