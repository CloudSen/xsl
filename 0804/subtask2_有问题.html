  1. Installation of the Pneumatic Starter.

    (Ref.Pneumatic Starter - Removal/Installation Figure 80-11-10-991-30200-06-A / SHEET 1 / SHEET 2)

    1. WARNING:BE CAREFUL WHEN YOU MOVE THE ENGINE STARTER. BECAUSE THE STARTER WEIGHS APPROXIMATELY 38 POUNDS (17 KG), INJURY CAN OCCUR.

      CAUTION:DURING INSTALLATION, KEEP THE STARTER PERPENDICULAR TO THE AGB MOUNTING PAD TO PREVENT SEIZE AND/OR DAMAGE TO THE OUTPUT DRIVE SHAFT.

      CAUTION:DO NOT INSTALL THE STARTER ON THE AGB WITHOUT ITS SEALING ITEMS (NEW O-RING AND THE RE-USED OR NEW SEAL RING). IF THE STARTER IS INSTALLED WITHOUT THESE SEALS, THE ENGINE CAN LEAK OIL. THIS CAN CAUSE ENGINE FAILURE.

      CAUTION:DURING INSTALLATION, ADJUST THE ORIENTATION OF THE LOWER DUCT. MAKE SURE THAT THE PS3 LINE DOES NOT TOUCH THE LOWER DUCT OR OTHER PARTS IN THIS AREA. IF IT DOES, CHAFING OF THE PS3 HOSE CAN OCCUR.

      CAUTION:DURING STARTER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION, BE CAREFUL NOT TO CAUSE DAMAGE TO THE STARTER FUSE PIN WHEN YOU TURN/TWIST THE STARTER ASSEMBLY IN OPPOSITE CLOCKWISE/COUNTERCLOCKWISE DIRECTIONS FROM THE MOUNTING PAD. CFM RECOMMENDS THAT YOU USE THE CORRECT LEVERAGE PROCEDURES AND CORRECT TOOLS TO REMOVE/INSTALL THE STARTER ASSEMBLY FROM/ON THE AGB.
